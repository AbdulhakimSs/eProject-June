About Alberto Watch Company

Founded in 1985, Alberto Watch Company has been a trusted name in luxury timepieces for over three decades. We specialize in watch repair, appraisal, and retail of the finest selection of luxury watches.

Our flagship store in Kampala, Uganda offers an unparalleled shopping experience with expert staff who are passionate about horology. We carry a wide selection of brand new watches from Michael Kors, Rolex, Citizen Eco-Drive, Bulova, and other premium brands.

At Alberto Watch Company, we believe that a fine timepiece is more than just an accessory - it's a legacy to be passed down through generations.
